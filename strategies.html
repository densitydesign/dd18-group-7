Green metaphors

The video

This supercut showcases some of the metaphors that appear in the analysed adverts. We handpicked the most relevant ones, put the two components of the comparisons side by side, synchronised them, and finally ordered them starting from the ones that are more related to nature in itself to the ones that refer to activities carried out in nature, to highlight recurrent patterns and clichés.

Characteristics

This strategy is about using common rhetorical techniques (metaphors) to associate the characteristics of the vehicle to nature—from animals to natural phenomena—and its driving to sports activities carried out in nature.

visual

Where

This strategy can be usually found in a sequence of two or more shots.
