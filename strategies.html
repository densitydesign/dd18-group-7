Green solutions

This strategy is about showing the vehicle as a possible solution or means to tackle climate change and reduce one's “carbon footprint” inside of a short story that revolves around the consequences or issues brought by climate change.

Green lifestyles

This strategy consists in the choice of the adverts' protagonists, who showcase some stereotyped green habits or their efforts to live more sustainably, in which the car itself plays a significant role.

Emissions and consumption statements

This strategy is about placing long paragraphs of text that detail the emissions and consumption information of the vehicle directly over the scenes of the advert itself or in particularly small areas or sections of it.
