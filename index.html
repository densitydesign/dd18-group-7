A collection of sustainability-related communication strategies in car adverts

Today, most car brands tend to use communication strategies that could be interpreted as a subtle way to associate their models and image to the concept of sustainability in their adverts on YouTube. So, we analysed 90 different adverts with the intention to see through the recurring patterns and identify them.
